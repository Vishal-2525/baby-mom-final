About Us

Dedicated To Nurturing Families with Quality, Care and Commitment

Welcome to Baby & Mom Retail Private Limited, your trusted partner in quality and care. We are dedicated to bringing joy, comfort, and convenience to families everywhere.

Founded on the principles of innovation and power of nature, our company specializes in providing top-notch consumer goods. We proudly support families with our seven diverse brands, each dedicated to providing safety, reliability, and excellence. From everyday essentials to unique solutions, we strive to nurture happiness, one product at a time.

Join us on this journey as we continue to grow, innovate, and make a positive impact on households around the world.

Tracing the Vision: Insights from Our Founder

Shish Kharesiya brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to Baby & Mom Retail Pvt. Ltd. With a background in retail & E- Commerce, he has combined his professional expertise with personal insights into parenthood to create a brand that truly understands the needs of modern families. Shish Kharesiya is dedicated to continually improving and expanding our product offerings, ensuring that we are always meeting the evolving needs of parents and their babies.

15+ Years of Industry Experience

Shish Kharesiya's extensive industry experience drives the development of practical, high-quality products that cater to the evolving needs of today's families.

D2C E-Commerce Specialist

By utilizing data insights along with marketplace expertise and innovative technology, he has enabled Baby & Mom Retail Pvt. Ltd. to deliver top-tier products directly to customers with unmatched convenience.

How Baby & Mom Company grew over the years.

Since its inception in 2013, Baby & Mom Retail Pvt. Ltd. has grown from a small team of passionate individuals to a global powerhouse with over 150+ employees.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, the expansion of our product lines, and the successful launch of seven vibrant brands.

Today, our products are cherished all over India, and we remain committed to crafting meaningful experiences that bring joy and care to families everywhere.
